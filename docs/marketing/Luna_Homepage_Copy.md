## Why Luna exists
In everyday life many states are difficult to read.

Fatigue.
Pressure.
Emotional overload.
Unclear signals from the body.

Luna helps make these states clearer through observation, reflection, and patterns that appear over time.

## How Luna works
### Your Body
physiological rhythms and markers

### Your Senses
observations and voice notes

### Your Words
clear and calm formulation of thoughts

Together this forms a clearer picture of your inner state.
