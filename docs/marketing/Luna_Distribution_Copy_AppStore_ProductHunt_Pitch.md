# Luna Distribution Copy Pack

## 1) App Store / Google Play

### App Name
Luna

### Subtitle (short)
Calm insight for women’s inner state

### Promotional Text
Understand your rhythm through body signals, daily reflections, and clear personal insights.

### Short Description
Luna helps women make sense of energy, mood, and body patterns with calm, private tools.

### Full Description
Luna is a personal understanding system for women.
It connects body rhythms, daily observations, and reflective language to help you see what is changing and why.

With Luna you can:
- map your rhythm visually,
- log voice reflections,
- notice recurring patterns,
- organize health markers,
- generate clear reports for doctor visits.

Luna is designed to feel calm, private, and human.
No overload. No dashboard stress. Just clear understanding over time.

### Keywords
women health, cycle, wellness, mood, energy, rhythm, hormonal health, self care, health report, voice journal
