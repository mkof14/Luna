# LUNA — One Pager (Users, Partners, Investors)

## What is Luna
Luna is a calm, premium digital space that helps women better understand their inner state by connecting body rhythms, lived observations, and clear language.

Luna is not another tracking app.
Luna is a personal understanding system: it turns daily signals into visible patterns and practical decisions.

## Problem
Many women experience recurring states that are hard to read in real time:
- low energy,
- emotional overload,
- focus fluctuations,
- unclear body signals.

Most tools either over-medicalize the experience or reduce it to basic logging.

## Solution
Luna combines structured observations, wellness markers, and report-ready insights in one private interface.

Core product modules:
- **Body Map**: visual rhythm and state map.
- **Ritual Path**: simple daily practice (morning, midday, evening).
- **Voice Journal**: capture reflections by voice, not only text.
- **The Bridge**: helps formulate your state in clear, calm language.
- **Health Reports**: transforms observations and lab data into understandable, structured reports.

## User Value
Luna helps users:
- see recurring patterns,
- understand triggers and timing,
- communicate better with partner/family/provider,
- arrive to consultations with clearer context.

## Partner Value
For clinics, wellness programs, and femtech partners, Luna provides:
- stronger user engagement,
- better pre-consultation clarity,
- structured user context,
- opportunities for co-created care and education programs.

## Business Potential
Luna is positioned at the intersection of:
- women’s health,
- emotional wellbeing,
- personalized digital care.

Model potential:
- B2C subscription,
- B2B/B2B2C partnerships,
- premium reporting services,
- future integrations with care ecosystems.

## Product Principles
- **Human-first UX**: calm, clear, non-clinical.
- **Privacy by design**: user-controlled personal data.
- **Clarity over noise**: less tracking overload, more interpretation.
- **Actionable insight**: practical meaning, not raw numbers only.

## Strategic Vision
Luna aims to become the personal intelligence layer for women’s daily state understanding — bridging lifestyle signals, emotional context, and report-ready structure.

## Brand Line
**Luna — personal intelligence for a woman’s inner state.**
